. Eiffel Tower

Identity: Eiffel Tower

Justification:

Tall metallic lattice structure, tapering silhouette.

No domes, no walls — simple skeletal iron tower.

Iconic “A-frame” shape, very different from Mughal or Tibetan architecture.

2. Taj Mahal

Identity: Taj Mahal

Justification:

Large white marble mausoleum. 

Central onion-shaped dome surrounded by smaller domes. 

Very high bilateral symmetry and formal planning. 

Ornamental arches (iwans) on each side. 


3. Potala Palace

Identity: Potala Palace

Justification:

Multi-level structure with flat roofs at different terraces. 

Distinct color scheme: white walls, red “Red Palace,” yellow/gold accents. 

Golden roofs / gilded canopies on top. 

Thick sloping walls, many windows, very fortress-like yet also palatial.
